Completed Projects
PacMen Factory

Each time the 'Add PacMan' button is pressed a new PacMan is created at a different place on the screen. Once you are satisfied with the amount of PacMen, the 'Start Game' button can be clicked. This will start the PacMen packing and bouncing in all directions at different velocities. WARNING: They will keep packing as long as you keep watching.

Live Bus Tracking

A project to track the live movement of a bus operating in the Boston area. The bus will be tracked every 15 seconds and the position will be updated. This will be shown by the marker that is displayed on the map. Click the 'Start Live Bus Tracking' button to get going.

Eye Movement

A basic project to track the movement of the mouse. When the mouse pointer is moved there are two eyes which both have black eye balls. These eye balls will track the movement of the mouse in unison.
